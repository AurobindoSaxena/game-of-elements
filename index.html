Game of Elements

Envisioned by Aurobindo Saxena

Rules of the Game

  • Elements: Fire, Water, Earth, Air, Ether.
  • Win Conditions: Fire beats Earth (burns) and Ether (purifies); Water beats Fire (extinguishes) and Earth (erodes); Earth beats Air (grounds) and Water (absorbs); Air beats Water (disrupts) and Ether (scatters); Ether beats Air (transcends) and Fire (controls).
  • Gameplay: 2-5 players join with unique names and secretly choose an element.
  • Outcome: A player wins if their element beats all others; draws occur if all choose the same element or no element dominates.
  • Rounds: Play multiple rounds; results show who chose what and why.
