Game of Elements

(Envisioned by Aurobindo Saxena)

Game Rules:

  • Fire: Beats Earth (burns it) and Ether (purifies it); but loses to Water (extinguished) and Air (disrupted).
  • Water: Beats Fire (extinguishes it) and Earth (erodes it); but loses to Air (disrupted) and Ether (transcended).
  • Earth: Beats Air (grounds it) and Water (absorbs it); but loses to Fire (burned) and Ether (transcended).
  • Air: Beats Water (disrupts it) and Ether (scatters it); but loses to Earth (grounded) and Fire (burned).
  • Ether: Beats Air (transcends it) and Fire (controls it); but loses to Water (eroded) and Earth (absorbed).
