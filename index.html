Game of Elements

Win Conditions:

  • Fire: Beats Earth (burns it), Ether (purifies it); Loses to Water (extinguished), Air (disrupted).
  • Water: Beats Fire (extinguishes it), Earth (erodes it); Loses to Air (disrupted), Ether (transcended).
  • Earth: Beats Air (grounds it), Water (absorbs it); Loses to Fire (burned), Ether (transcended).
  • Air: Beats Water (disrupts it), Ether (scatters it); Loses to Earth (grounded), Fire (burned).
  • Ether: Beats Air (transcends it), Fire (controls it); Loses to Water (eroded), Earth (absorbed).
