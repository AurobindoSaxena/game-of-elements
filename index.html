Game of Elements

(Envisioned by Aurobindo Saxena)

Game Rules:

  • Fire: Beats Earth and Ether; loses to Water and Air.
  • Water: Beats Fire and Earth; loses to Air and Ether.
  • Earth: Beats Air and Water; loses to Fire and Ether.
  • Air: Beats Water and Ether; loses to Earth and Fire.
  • Ether: Beats Air and Fire; loses to Water and Earth.
